Welcome to Lemon Grove Bicycles, where two-wheel dreams come to life! Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, our bike shop is more than just a retail space—it's a haven for cycling enthusiasts and beginners alike. As you step through our doors, the vibrant atmosphere is palpable, echoing the hum of tires on the open road.

Our dedicated team of cycling experts is committed to providing personalized service, guiding you through a vast selection of top-notch bicycles for all terrains and preferences. From sleek road bikes engineered for speed demons to rugged mountain bikes built to conquer any trail, we offer a diverse range of brands and models to suit every rider.

But our commitment doesn't end with sales; we're here for the long ride. At Lemon Grove Bicycles, we take pride in offering comprehensive services to keep your bike in peak condition. Our skilled technicians provide professional repairs, tune-ups, and customizations, ensuring your trusty steed is always ready for the next adventure.

Additionally, we host community events, group rides, and workshops to foster a thriving cycling culture. Whether you're a seasoned cyclist or a casual rider, Lemon Grove Bicycles is your destination for quality bikes, expert advice, and a community that shares your love for the open road. Pedal with us and let the journey unfold!
